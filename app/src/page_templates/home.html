May this occasion shower us with many memories of James A Gresham.

On the morning of Tuesday, March 26th, 2024 James "Chipper" transition into the arms of the Lord.

Chipper was named by his Nana before he was born. He and his Nana shared deep love for one another. Chipper was a protective, adventurous, and compassionate soul. Chipper embodied compassion, if you needed anything he would gladly give you whatever he had, most time his last.

Chipper was a lover of science, music, and animals. He was a free loving spirit, who enjoyed life to the fullest, and lived it on his terms.

We will forever cherish his memory of our times together.

April 27, 1963 - March 26, 2024

Hosted By First Baptist Church Bronxville

MC'd by Rev Lamont S. Granby
